The Burden of Travel

While traveling for knowledge is a noble and highly rewarded endeavor in Islam, it presents significant practical challenges for the modern family. Uprooting children, leaving careers, and adjusting to completely different cultures and educational systems abroad is not a viable option for the vast majority of Canadian Muslims. Consequently, many highly capable students were forced to settle for sub-par local education.
