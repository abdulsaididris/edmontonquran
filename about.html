Our Director

Sheikh Mohamed Moamen

Expertise & Sanad

High-Level Sanad · Edmonton Alberta

Sheikh Mohamed Moamen is a distinguished Quranic scholar based in Edmonton, dedicated to preserving and transmitting the highest standards of Quranic recitation and memorization to the next generation.

He holds a high-level Sanad — an unbroken chain of transmission connecting directly to the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — ensuring that every student who studies under him receives the purest, most authentic form of Quranic recitation.

Sheikh Moamen personally oversees every student's daily memorization assignments, ensuring no one advances without demonstrating true mastery at the highest calibre.

Former Student of Dr. Ahmad Essa El Masarawi

Sheikh Moamen is a proud former student of the globally esteemed Dr. Ahmad Essa El Masarawi — renowned Egyptian scholar, premier Quraa expert, and former Grand Sheikh of the Reciters in Egypt (2004–2014). Dr. Masarawi is universally recognized as a top expert in Tajweed and the ten Qiraa-aat, celebrated for his contributions to Quranic studies and his teaching role at Al-Azhar University.
